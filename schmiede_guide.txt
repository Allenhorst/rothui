  Overall you need 220 Cobalt bars, 455 Saronite bars and 20 Eternal Shadow.
  You will craft 65 items in that process. 
  
  The smith in Dalaran will sell all the recipes needed. 
  So just gather all the materials first, then rush through the process in one step.
  
  Num       = Number of items that need to be crafted
  Cobalt    = Cobalt Bar      (http://wotlk.wowhead.com/?item=36916)
  Saronite  = Saronite Bar    (http://wotlk.wowhead.com/?item=36913)
  ES        = Eternal Shadow  (http://wotlk.wowhead.com/?item=35627)
